Webster defines bountiful like this:

Adjective: given or provided abudantly



One Utah city defines Bountiful like this:

Hometown: giving or providing abudantly

Inspiring definitions for a town and an outlook on life. As a life-long advocate for both, I've posted some of my own definitions of bountiful and Bountiful below. I hope that you will take a few moments to do the same.

Why? Because what qualifies as bountiful to you will likely offer hope and appreciation to somebody who is having a difficult time finding the bounty of their own life in their own town.
